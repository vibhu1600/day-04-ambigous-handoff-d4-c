# 3-Minute Stakeholder Update Video

Maximum length: 3 minutes.

This is not a presentation. This is the update you would give before execution starts.

## Required Structure

### 0:00-0:30 - Decision

State:

- your assigned packet
- Proceed / Proceed conditionally / Block pending clarification
- one-sentence reason

### 0:30-1:10 - Ambiguity

Explain:

- what was unclear
- why it mattered
- what could go wrong if ignored

### 1:10-1:50 - Clarification and Assumptions

Explain:

- your one clarification question
- founder response
- assumptions you still own

### 1:50-2:30 - Minimal Safe V1

Explain:

- what you would build now
- what you would not build
- why the rejected scope is unsafe or excessive now

### 2:30-3:00 - Founder Update

Give the exact update you would send to the founder.

## Strong Video

- sounds like an engineer protecting execution quality
- identifies ambiguity before solution
- communicates risk without panic
- rejects overbuild clearly
- owns a decision

## Weak Video

- says "I would build the feature"
- repeats the template
- hides assumptions
- treats AI output as final
- blocks everything without a useful next step
